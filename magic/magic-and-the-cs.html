The World
Magic and the Cs

Magic is a common occurrence in this world, known but not usable by everyone. Certain species are more prone to having an affinity for a certain C than others.

The names of each of the 'elements' in this world all start with a C, which in turn leads to the common questions of asking others "What's your C?" or "Do you have a C?".

Simply having a C does not reflect your proficiency - having an affinity does not make one an active practitioner. The Cs can be nurtured and cultivated over time with practice.

The Seven Cs

  1. Cat - Magic derived from observing Cats, as they have a natural affinity for this kind of magic. Cat magic is the most commonly found, as not only is it the easiest to learn without aid, but it is also abundant as Cats can be found across the World.
  2. Citrus - Magic relating to acids, citrus fruits and corrosion.
  3. Crystal - Magic relating to minerals, earth and light.
  4. Chemical - Magic relating to nature, alchemy and poison.
  5. Chilli - Magic relating to the chthonic realm and heat.
  6. Cool - Magic relating to coldness, calmness and numbness.
  7. Comet - Magic relating to space, gravity and stardust.

Flavours

Each C has an associated flavour. Generally, one of the common ways of honing one's proficiency with a C is to eat food with the associated flavour, though this is not enough in a vacuum and only works as a supplement to other practices.

Flavour Associations:

  • Cat - Bland
  • Citrus - Sour
  • Crystal - Salty
  • Chemical - Bitter
  • Chilli - Spicy
  • Cool - Minty
  • Comet - Sweet

Other Cs

Clown and Cheese are two of the most common 'joke' Cs that some people occasionally claim to have an affinity for. These Cs are mostly said in jest, typically by those who do not have an affinity for any C. Certain groups of people have gone on to fabricate extensive lore regarding these Cs.

Other fake Cs also exist, but are not as prominently widespread as Clown and Cheese.
